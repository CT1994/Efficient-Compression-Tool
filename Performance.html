Performance

All tests were done on a mid 2011 iMac running OS X 10.10 using an Intel i5-2500S. The test images are the images on the frontpages of the Alexa Top 25 sites, the GZIP test files the compressed responses for the HTML file.
Mode Compression Time
PNG -M1 -strip 713.76kB out of 5.79MB (12.05%) 16.36s
PNG -M2 -strip 916.60KB out of 5.79MB (15.47%) 2m 17.7s
PNG -M3 -strip 985.12KB out of 5.79MB (16.62%) 4m 38.4s
PNG -M4 -strip 1011.42KB out of 5.79MB (17.07%) 12m 10.5s
JPEG -M1 -strip -progressive 447.25KB out of 4.64MB (9.40%) 3.34s
GZIP 32.43KB out of 542.58KB (5.98%) 4.06s
GZIP -M3 34.76KB out of 542.58KB (6.41%) 8.39s
GZIP -M4 35.00KB out of 542.58KB (6.45%) 14.29s

Decoding time usually stagnates. The only option changing this is -progressive which increases JPEG decoding time.

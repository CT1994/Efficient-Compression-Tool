Performance

All tests were done on a mid 2011 iMac running OS X 10.10 using an Intel i5-2500S. The test images are the images on the frontpages of the Alexa Top 25 sites.
Mode Compression Time
PNG -M1 -strip 713.76kB out of 5.79MB (12.05%) 16.5s
PNG -M2 -strip 892.81kB out of 5.79MB (15.07%) 7m 7.31s
PNG -M3 -strip 958.08kB out of 5.79MB (16.17%) 10m 27.07s
PNG -M4 -strip 1.01MB out of 5.79MB (16.64%) 18m 56.04s
JPEG -M1 -strip -progressive 446.94kB out of 4.64MB (9.40%) 3.34s

Decoding time usually stagnates. The only option changing this is -progressive which increases JPEG decoding time.
